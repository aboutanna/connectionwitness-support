Connection Witness — Support

Connection Witness is a lightweight menu bar app for macOS that monitors your internet connection in real time.

Need help, found a bug, or have a question? Email us at annahughtes02@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Reporting a purchase issue

If your problem involves the one-time unlock purchase (e.g. it's not being recognized, or "Restore Purchases" isn't working), please include the following in your email so we can help faster:

  • A screenshot of your App Store purchase receipt or order confirmation (from your email, or Apple ID → Purchase History)
  • The Apple ID you used to make the purchase
  • A short description of what happened
