Connection Witness — Privacy Policy

Connection Witness runs entirely on your Mac. It does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data, usage data, or analytics.

The app periodically sends network requests to a target you choose (or a default endpoint) solely to test connectivity. No data from these checks ever leaves your device or is sent to us — all history and statistics are stored locally on your Mac.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit; we never see your payment information.
